Science is the explination of everything in the universe. Different sciences explain different information about the universe. These sciences are seperated into the many branches of science.

The Branches of SCIENCE

There are many branches of science but first you must learn about the three main sciences.

  • Physical Science: deals with matter and energy and allows us to describe. them.
  • Earth Science: explains the Earth, its atmosphere, and the solar system to which it belongs.
  • Life Science: discribes living organisms thier internal processes and there relationship to each other in the enviroment.

As I said these three sciences are seperated into the many branches of science.
